Dua for Beginning of Ramadan

اللَّهُمَّ أَهِلَّهُ عَلَيْنَا بِالأَمْنِ وَالإِيمَانِ، وَالسَّلامَةِ وَالإِسْلامِ، وَالتَّوْفِيقِ لِمَا تُحِبُّ وَتَرْضَى.
"O Allah, bring it upon us with security and faith, safety and Islam, and success in what You love and are pleased with."

Dua for Beginning of Ramadan

اللَّهُمَّ أَهِلَّهُ عَلَيْنَا بِالأَمْنِ وَالإِيمَانِ، وَالسَّلامَةِ وَالإِسْلامِ، وَالتَّوْفِيقِ لِمَا تُحِبُّ وَتَرْضَى.
"O Allah, bring it upon us with security and faith, safety and Islam, and success in what You love and are pleased with."

Dua for Breaking the Fast (Iftar)

ذَهَبَ الظَّمَأُ، وَابْتَلَّتِ العُرُوقُ، وَثَبَتَ الأَجْرُ إِنْ شَاءَ اللَّهُ.
"The thirst is gone, the veins are moistened, and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills."

Dua for Laylatul Qadr

اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ العَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي.
"O Allah, You are the Pardoner, and You love to pardon, so pardon me."

Dua for Forgiveness

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَارْحَمْنِي وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الرَّاحِمِينَ.
"O my Lord, forgive me and have mercy on me, for You are the best of the merciful."

Dua for Guidance

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ.
"Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the punishment of the Fire."

Dua for Patience

رَبِّ أَعِنِّي وَلا تُعِنْ عَلَيَّ، وَانْصُرْنِي وَلا تَنْصُرْ عَلَيَّ.
"O my Lord, help me and do not help others against me, and grant me victory and do not grant others victory over me."

Dua for Gratitude

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي هَدَانَا لِهَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لِنَهْتَدِيَ لَوْلا أَنْ هَدَانَا اللَّهُ.
"Praise be to Allah who has guided us to this, and we would not have been guided if Allah had not guided us."

Dua for Protection from Hellfire

اللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنَا مِنَ النَّارِ.
"O Allah, save us from the Fire."

Dua for Increasing Knowledge

رَبِّ زِدْنِي عِلْمًا.
"O my Lord, increase me in knowledge."

Dua for Parents

رَبِّ اغْفِرْ لِي وَلِوَالِدَيَّ وَلِلْمُؤْمِنِينَ يَوْمَ يَقُومُ الحِسَابُ.
"O my Lord, forgive me, my parents, and the believers on the Day of Reckoning."

Dua for Family

رَبَّنَا هَبْ لَنَا مِنْ أَزْوَاجِنَا وَذُرِّيَّاتِنَا قُرَّةَ أَعْيُنٍ وَاجْعَلْنَا لِلْمُتَّقِينَ إِمَامًا.
"Our Lord, grant us from among our spouses and offspring comfort to our eyes and make us an example for the righteous."

Dua for the Ummah

اللَّهُمَّ اشْفِ مَرْضَانَا وَمَرْضَى أُمَّةِ مُحَمَّدٍ.
"O Allah, heal our sick and the sick among the Ummah of Muhammad."

Dua for Unity

اللَّهُمَّ وَحِّدْ كَلِمَةَ المُسْلِمِينَ.
"O Allah, unite the hearts of the Muslims."

Dua for Repentance

أَسْتَغْفِرُ اللَّهَ وَأَتُوبُ إِلَيْهِ.
"I seek forgiveness from Allah and repent to Him."

Dua for Strength

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ بِرَحْمَتِكَ الَّتِي وَسِعَتْ كُلَّ شَيْءٍ أَنْ تَغْفِرَ لِي.
"O Allah, I ask You by Your mercy that encompasses everything to forgive me."

Dua for Eid

اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، لا إِلَهَ إِلا اللَّهُ، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، وَلِلَّهِ الحَمْدُ.
"Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest, there is no god but Allah, Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest, and to Allah belongs all praise."

Dua for Fasting

اللهم لك صمت وعلى رزقك أفطرت.
"O Allah, I have fasted for You, and with Your provision, I break my fast."

Dua for Breaking the Fast

ذهب الظمأ وابتلت العروق وثبت الأجر إن شاء الله.
"Thirst is gone, the veins are moistened, and the reward is confirmed, if Allah wills."

Dua for Laylatul Qadr

اللهم إنك عفو تحب العفو فاعف عني.
"O Allah, You are Most Forgiving, and You love forgiveness, so forgive me."
