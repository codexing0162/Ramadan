Breaking the Fast with Dates or Water

"The Prophet (ﷺ) used to fast and break his fast with dates, and if there were no dates, he would break his fast with water." (Sunan Abu Dawood 2356)

Forgiveness of Sins for Fasting with Faith

"Whoever fasts in Ramadan with faith and seeking reward, all his past sins will be forgiven." (Sahih Bukhari 38)

Fasting as a Shield from the Fire

"Fasting is a shield with which a servant protects himself from the fire." (Sahih Bukhari 6669)

Gate of Ar-Rayyan in Paradise

"There is a gate in Paradise called Ar-Rayyan, and those who fast will enter through it on the Day of Judgment." (Sahih Bukhari 1896)

Increased Worship During the Last Ten Nights

"The Prophet (ﷺ) used to strive more in worship during the last ten nights of Ramadan." (Sahih Bukhari 2024)

Forgiveness for Forgetting to Eat or Drink

"If anyone eats or drinks forgetfully, then let him complete his fast, for what he has eaten or drunk has been given to him by Allah." (Sahih Bukhari 6669)

Virtue of the Last Ten Days of Ramadan

"There are no days in which good deeds are more beloved to Allah than in these ten days (the last ten days of Ramadan)." (Sahih Bukhari 2023)

Opening of Heaven and Closing of Hell During Ramadan

"When Ramadan begins, the gates of Heaven are opened, and the gates of Hell are closed, and the devils are chained." (Sahih Muslim 1079)

Forgiveness for Praying at Night During Ramadan

"Whoever prays during the night of Ramadan with faith and seeking reward, will be forgiven for his past sins." (Sahih Bukhari 38)
